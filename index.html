About Our Team

Learn more about our history and what makes us unique

Heppy Minecraft Team was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate Minecraft players who wanted to take their skills to the next level. What started as a small group of friends has grown into one of the most respected competitive Minecraft teams in the world.

Our team specializes in three main areas: competitive PvP tournaments, large-scale collaborative builds, and technical redstone creations. We've won multiple championships in each of these categories and continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in Minecraft.

What sets us apart is our commitment to teamwork and continuous improvement. Every member of Heppy brings unique skills to the table, and we work together to help each other grow.
